struct units\+::unknown\+\_\+dimension$<$ Es $>$}
Unknown dimension. 

Sometimes a temporary partial result of a complex calculation may not result in a predefined dimension. In such a case an {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{structunits_1_1unknown__dimension}{unknown\+\_\+dimension}}} is created with a coherent unit of {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{structunits_1_1unknown__coherent__unit}{unknown\+\_\+coherent\+\_\+unit}}} with a magnitude being the absolute one of all the exponents of such a dimension.
