A prefix used to scale units. 

Data from a prefix class is used in two cases\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item when defining a \mbox{\hyperlink{structunits_1_1prefixed__unit}{prefixed\+\_\+unit}} its ratio is used to scale the reference unit and its symbol is used to prepend to the symbol of referenced unit
\item when printing the symbol of a scaled unit that was not predefined by the user but its factor matches ratio of a prefix, its symbol will be prepended to the symbol of the unit
\end{DoxyItemize}
